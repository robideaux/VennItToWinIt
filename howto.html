How to play

Select a game from the pull-down menu. This will reveal seven phrases.
Click and place the phrases to the correct area in the Venn diagram.
Hit the Check button to check your answers.
If all the correct pharses are in a circle the title of the category will be revealed;
even if they are misplaced within the circle.
Reveal all the categories before checking four times to win the game

Sharing a game

To share a game to chllenge your friends,
click the "hamburger icon" on the right side of the screen and click "Share current game".
A url from the current game will be copied to your clipboard and can be pasted in a text, email, or social media post.

Create your own game

To modify a game or create your own challenge,
click the "hamburger icon" on the right side of the screen and click "Edit game".
If you have already selected a game, you will be able to change the title, phrases, and categories.
If you have not yet selected a game, you will be give a blank template to create your own.
When you are finished, click the green Save button so save it to your cache.
Your game will now appear is the drop down with the tag "(local copy)".
You can select it and share it with your friends.
